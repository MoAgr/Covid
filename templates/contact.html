About App:

Our aim is to provide people with easy access to one of the most important data during this time. We also aim to provide a reliable source through our website for access of data. Important links to various portals are highlighted for the user, along with links to free resources to practise from during the lockdown period. Our platform enables the user to get timely information, news and resources. We will be updating it with new features in the near future.
